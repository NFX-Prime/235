Soda Popper!

High Concept

Soda Popper is a game that is similar to Root Beer Tapper, a game where you play as a bartender serving drinks to customers.
For my concept, I would like to attempt to recreate Tapper, but you play as a waiter serving soda cans to a busy college party.

Genre

The genre of the game is considered action by Wikipedia, since it revolves around tossing soda at college kids

Platform

The game will be desktop only

Story

The main story of Soda Popper:

You will be playing as a waiter who got scheduled for the busiest night at their workplace. Today is the big game, and lots of college students are coming
to get some grub, and a nice bottle of pop to sip on.

The main motivation of the player should be giving soda cans to the customers that are in the line!

Aesthetics

Since it's based on Tapper, I was planning on using pixel art graphics with an 8-bit sound design. The types of sounds the player should hear are
background music, maybe happy dings if they successfully serve a customer / collect cash, and a dissatisfying noise if they miss a customer.

Gameplay

Mechanics

Some of the main mechanics that the game has are the following:

Moving up and down a set of lines of customers, tossing soda cans down the lines to customers, walking down the line to collect cash

Controls

  • W and S: Move up/down the lines
  • A: Move into the line
  • M1: Toss soda cans into the line

Onboarding

There will be small tips on the start menu to tell the player how they can move and serve customers

Player Learning

The player needs to learn how to properly manage their position so they can successfully serve the customers.
They will also learn to manage how many sodas they throw at once, because if they throw too many, they will miss the customers and lose a life.
