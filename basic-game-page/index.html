Lovers in a Dangerous Spacetime

Publishing Info

  • Published by: Asteroid Base Inc. & Super Rare Games Limited
  • Developed by: Asteroid Base Inc.
  • Released: Sep 9th, 2015

Description

Lovers in a Dangerous Spacetime is a 4 player co-op game where you control a giant spaceship!
Inside of the spaceship, there are multiple different controllable parts, such as a shield, weapons, and the engine that all must be controlled by the players.
The goal of the game is to save the 5 captured creatures around the planet, and to defeat the Anti-Love forces surrounding them.
There are many combinations for weapons, as well as space crystals that can upgrade your ship!

Game Categories

  • Genre: Action
  • Perspective: Side View
  • Visual: Fantasy
  • Gameplay: Shooter
