Project 1 Documentation

The purpose of this document is to make a small website about a very popular indie game called ULTRAKILL, a game about a robot fighting through the layers of hell to eliminate everything inside of it. I found this game to also be very fun, which is why I decided to make my project about it.

The audience of this document are the people (like me) who enjoy playing the game ULTRAKILL, as well as others who may be interested in the premise of the game and its mechanics. With that in mind, I wanted the information to be quite organized (unlike the game itself) to give a comprehensive view of the story of ULTRAKILL and some reasons why I personally enjoyed it.
