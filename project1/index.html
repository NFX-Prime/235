ULTRAKILL is a game developed by Hakita, and is published by the studio New Blood Interactive. The game is a member of the Boomer Shooter genre, similar to games like Doom Eternal and Dusk (Which is also developed by New Blood!). Inside the game you play as V1, one of the machines who enters HELL for the sole purpose of living through the absorption of BLOOD. Throughout the game, you enter the various layers of HELL to face countless enemies and challenges to sustain your literal BLOODTHIRST using your variety of guns and arm types that you gain throughout your journey.

Arms come in three types (as of now), which can swap between a blue arm to parry hell energy projectiles, a red arm that is essentially a shotgun, and a green arm that acts as a grappling hook. Enemies you face come in varying types as well, such as the HUSKS and DEMONS of hell, the angels to keep them in place, and even other MACHINES with the same directives, and you as their target. With all of this information, there are also some other gameplay mechanics to touch on, such as the prominent Style Meter. The Style Meter is measured by how flashy you play. Something like punching an enemy to death is kind of stylish, but blowing them up into mist is VERY stylish. It's also how you gain style points to acquire your very flexible loadout as you go deeper down the layers. But just know, after GOD'S disappearance from heaven, you aren't the only sentient being within the unholy confines of this place. Prominent figures of HELL have been sealed for eons, and you're looking for a CHALLENGE.

Final Notes

Just as a personal note, ULTRAKILL is one of the many games that I have enjoyed playing. I consider it one of my favorite indie games of all time because of how well the artstyle fits with the almost breakneck speed of the gameplay. It also really boosted my drive to continue trying to make games, and maybe to make a game that is just as fun and exciting as this one was for me.
